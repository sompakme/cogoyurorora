Yuki Kaji Career

He has voiced many roles, such as Shion in No. 6, Eren Yeager in Attack on Titan, Lyon Vastia in Fairy Tail, Issei Hyodo in High School DxD, Shu Ouma in Guilty Crown, Alibaba in Magi: The Labyrinth of Magic, Kazuya Ichinose and Akio Fudou in Inazuma Eleven: Orion no Kokuin, Citron (Clemont in English dub) in Pokémon the Series: XY, Meliodas and Zeldris in The Seven Deadly Sins, Yukine in Noragami, Speed-O’Sound Sonic in One-Punch Man, Kanato Sakamaki in Diabolik Lovers, Finnian in Black Butler, Kozume Kenma in Haikyu!!, Mei Narumiya in Ace of Diamond, Kakeru Yuiga in Norn9, Shoto Todoroki in My Hero Academia, Johnny Joestar in JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure: Eyes of Heaven, Koichi Hirose in JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure, Izana Kurokawa in Tokyo Revengers, and Nishikata in Teasing Master Takagi-san.

He was a member of the four-member singing ensemble G.Addict, which was part of the Goulart Knights project, and he played one of the main characters in the film Kami Voice: The Voice Makes a Miracle. In 2009, he received the Best Rookie Actor Award at the 3rd Seiyu Awards.

He won the Best Voice Actor Award two years in a row at the 7th and 8th Seiyu Awards, in 2013 and 2014. Since March 2014, he has presented the web radio show Yki Kaji’s Monologue ( Kaji Yki no Hitorigoto). In April 2017, his variety show Kaji 100!: The 100 Things Yuki Kaji Wants to Do premiered on the Nitteleplus channel.
