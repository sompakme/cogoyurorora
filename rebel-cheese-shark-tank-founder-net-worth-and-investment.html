The Story Behind Rebel Cheese

Kirsten Maitland and Fred Zwar, a husband-and-wife team, are the founders of Rebel Cheese, a renowned brand in the vegan cheese industry. What started as a personal journey towards veganism quickly transformed into a mission to create mouthwatering, plant-based cheeses that could rival their dairy counterparts.

Driven by Kirsten’s overnight decision to give up cheese after learning about the health risks associated with dairy products, the couple embarked on a mission to develop their own line of artisan vegan cheeses. Collaborating with dairy specialists, they honed their craft and perfected their recipes to create a range of exceptional vegan cheeses.

Rebel Cheese’s artisan cheeses are crafted from simple yet flavorful ingredients like cashews, water, salt, and cultures. Through careful experimentation and dedication to their craft, Kirsten and Fred achieved a creamy texture and mouthwatering taste that rival traditional dairy cheeses. But what sets Rebel Cheese apart is its commitment to being cholesterol-free and entirely plant-based, making it a healthier and cruelty-free alternative.

For Kirsten and Fred, Rebel Cheese is more than just a business; it’s a mission to provide people with delicious and healthy alternatives to traditional dairy products. Their creations appeal not only to vegans but also to those seeking sustainable and ethical food options that don’t compromise on taste and quality.

Rebel Cheese’s Response to COVID-19

As the COVID-19 pandemic swept across the globe, businesses had to quickly adapt to the new normal. Rebel Cheese, a renowned vegan cheese brand, was no exception. With a strong commitment to serving their customers and maintaining a consistent customer experience, Rebel Cheese swiftly implemented several changes to ensure their operations continued smoothly.

One of the key adjustments Rebel Cheese made was shifting their operations online. Recognizing the importance of e-commerce in the current landscape, they revamped their website and launched online operations to cater to their customers’ needs. This transition allowed them to reach a wider audience and offer their products to cheese enthusiasts nationwide.

To make up for the lack of in-person cheese sampling, Rebel Cheese came up with a creative solution: virtual cheese sampling. Leveraging technology, they brought the tasting experience directly to their customers’ homes. Through interactive online sessions, cheese lovers could explore the unique flavors and textures of Rebel Cheese’s artisan vegan cheeses, guided by knowledgeable cheese experts.

“Even though we couldn’t physically gather for cheese tasting events, we wanted to create an immersive and engaging experience for our customers. Virtual cheese sampling allowed us to connect with cheese enthusiasts from all over while providing them the opportunity to discover the deliciousness of our cheeses,” said Kirsten Maitland, co-founder of Rebel Cheese.

Another significant change Rebel Cheese made was the relocation of their manufacturing facility. By moving to a larger space, they optimized their production capacity and streamlined their operations. This move not only allowed for increased productivity but also resulted in significant cost savings, which were then passed on to their valued customers.

Throughout these challenging times, Rebel Cheese remained unwavering in their commitment to meet the increasing demand for their products. By quickly adapting their operations to an online model, introducing virtual cheese sampling, and optimizing their manufacturing facility, Rebel Cheese demonstrated resilience and innovation in the face of adversity.

Rebel Cheese’s response to the COVID-19 pandemic showcased their dedication to providing exceptional vegan cheeses to their customers while navigating the uncertainties of the global crisis. Their ability to adapt to the evolving landscape is a testament to their commitment to customer satisfaction and their position as a leader in the vegan cheese industry.
